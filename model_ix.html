Interior & Technology

YMX Curved Display

A seamless, single-piece curved glass surface housing both the 12.3" instrument and 14.9" control displays.

Shy Tech Philosophy

Intelligent technology discretely integrated into the design, appearing only when needed, such as hidden speakers.

Electrochromic Panoramic Roof

The Sky Lounge roof can switch from transparent to opaque instantly with a button press for shading.

Hexagonal Steering Wheel

A unique, non-circular steering wheel design that enhances visibility and provides a futuristic feel.

Integrated iDrive Controller

Crystal glass controls on the center console, including the gear selector and iconic iDrive controller.

Natural Interaction

Advanced voice and gesture control allows for interaction with the vehicle's functions without touching a screen.

Exterior & Driving Dynamics

Fifth-Gen eDrive Technology

Highly efficient electric motors, power electronics, and high-voltage battery providing impressive range and performance.

Self-Healing Kidney Grille

The intelligent kidney grille houses camera technology and sensors and features a self-repairing surface for minor scratches.

YMX Iconic Sounds Electric

A unique, emotionally rich acoustic feedback developed by composer Hans Zimmer for the driving experience.

Integrated Flush Door Handles

Door handles are flush with the bodywork, enhancing the clean aerodynamic design and efficiency.

Air Suspension & IAS

Optional Integral Active Steering (rear-wheel steering) and two-axle air suspension for superior agility and comfort.

Lightweight Construction

Use of Carbon Cage technology (CFRP) in the body structure to offset the battery weight and increase rigidity.
